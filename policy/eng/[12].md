# Catalogue for the Guidance of Foreign Investment Industries (2007 Revision)

*(Promulgated by Order No. 57 of the National Development and Reform Commission and the Ministry of Commerce on October 31, 2007; effective as of December 1, 2007)*

------

## Encouraged Foreign Investment Industries

### VII. Financial Industry

1. Banks, financial leasing companies, finance companies, trust and investment companies, and money brokerage firms.
2. Insurance companies (for life insurance companies, the foreign shareholding ratio shall not exceed 50%).
3. Securities companies (limited to underwriting of A-shares; and the underwriting and trading of B-shares, H-shares, and government and corporate bonds; the foreign shareholding ratio shall not exceed one-third) and securities investment fund management companies (the foreign shareholding ratio shall not exceed 49%).
4. Insurance brokerage companies.
5. Futures companies (with Chinese parties holding a controlling interest).
