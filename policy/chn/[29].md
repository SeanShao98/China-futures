Address from President

2010 年，在中国证监会正确领导下，我国期货市场继续保持

了良好的发展势头。期货市场基础性法规制度进一步完善，市场

规模稳步扩大，股指期货平稳推出，风险防范和处置能力显著提

高，服务国民经济发展的功能日益显现，初步具备了在更高层面

加快发展、更好服务国民经济发展的基础和条件。

In 2010, under the correct leadership of China Security

Regulatory Commission (CSRC), Chinese futures market continued a

positive trend of development. With further improvement in

fundamental laws and regulations of the futures market, steadily

expansion of market share, successful launch of the HS300 stock

index futures as well as remarkable improvement in risk management

capabilities, the function of futures market to serve national economic

development has become more and more important, Chinese futures

market has gained the foundation of more rapid development at a

higher stage and better serving the national economic development.

在过去的一年里，郑州商品交易所各项工作也取得了积极成

效。健全市场一线监管工作制度，稳妥化解市场潜在风险，守住

了不发生任何风险的底线；积极创新市场培育方式，深化功能发

挥研究，服务实体经济能力不断增强；建立完善信息技术系统工作制度，强化运维管理，技术保障水平显著提高；加大高素质人

才队伍建设力度，强化内部管理，发展基础日益牢固。

During the past year, Zhengzhou Commodity Exchange (ZCE)

has made positive progress in different aspects. It further enhanced

work-discipline for front-line surveillance, reliably eliminated

potential market risks, hence successfully kept the “zero risk

tolerance” bottom line; It actively involved in innovation of market

cultivation, deepened the functions of futures market and exerted its

advantages in research, strengthened its ability to serve the real

economy; It set up and improved work guideline for information

technology system, emphasized on management of operations and

maintenance, hence improved technical support standard; It intensified

the efforts to build-up talented workforce, reinforced on internal

management, and consolidated the foundation for further

development.

2011 年是我国“十二五”规划的开局之年，也将是期货市场从

量的扩张到质的提升转变关键的一年。在新的一年里，郑商所将

按照中央关于稳步发展期货市场的精神，贯彻落实全国证券期货

监管会议部署，以科学发展为主题，以服务实体经济为宗旨，进

一步修订和完善交易制度，方便产业链企业利用期货市场；加强

对期货市场服务“三农”工作的总结，健全农产品期货服务“三农”的

机制和模式；强化市场一线监管，保障期货市场价格发现和管理风险基础功能正常发挥；加强已上市品种培育，推动新品种研发

上市；启动新一期交易系统开发工作，坚持信息技术系统建设优

先战略。同时，加大市场宣传和舆论引导，深化社会各界对期货

市场功能与作用的认识，为期货市场发展创造良好的外部环境，

努力在新的一年里，以更加优异的成绩迎接建党 90 周年！

2011 marks the start of the twelfth “Five-Year Plan” and it also

represents that Chinese futures market is standing at a crucial turning

point from quantitative expansion to qualitative improvement. In the

coming year, ZCE shall follow the instructions made by the CCCPC

on steady development of futures market, conduct arrangements made

during the “National Securities and Futures Surveillance Conference”

,

follow the route of Scientific Development with the purpose of better

serving the real economy, further revise and improve trading practices,

make it easier for enterprises within industrial chains to enter into

futures market; Focus more on summarizing the experiences of futures

market serves “Agriculture, Rural areas and Farmers Issues”, improve

its mechanism and pattern; Intensify front-line market surveillance,

ensure the basic functions of futures market on price discovery and

risk management are properly implemented. Improve the cultivation

of existing commodities, promote R&D and listing of new

commodities. Initiate the development of new trading system,

prioritize the development of information technology system.

Meanwhile, intensify efforts on market promotion and direct public

opinions, improve the recognition of public on functions and roles offutures market, hence create a comfortable external environment for

the development of the futures market, and work harder in the coming

year, warmly welcome the 90th birthday of the Communist Party of

China with a better result.

二、 市场发展

Market Development

2010 年，是郑商所实现加快发展的一年。全年共成交期货合

约 49582.54 万手，成交金额 308956.65 亿元，较上年分别增长

118.36%和 223.37%。过去的一年，郑商所服务实体经济水平进一

步提高。深入开展“期货服务三农”系列活动，启动并建成集宣传、

研究、评估于一体的“点基地”、“面基地”工程，初步构建了多方协作、

共同参与的市场大服务格局，不断拓展期货市场服务实体经济的

广度和深度。成功举办首届“郑州农产品期货（棉花）高峰论坛”，取得了深化已上市品种培育、促进市场功能发挥的积极效果。强

化市场一线监管，保证了市场总体平稳运行。

In 2010, Zhengzhou Commodity Exchange achieved rapid

development. Total trading volume and turnover for the year were

495.8254m contracts and 30,895.665 bn Yuan, with year-on-year

growth rates of 118.36% and 223.37% respectively. In the past year,

ZCE has further improved its ability to serve the real economy. It

launched a series of programs on “Futures Market Serves Agriculture,

Rural areas and Farmers Issues”, started-up the promotion, research

and evaluation process on “typical individual and general cases of

how futures market serves the real economy and agriculture, rural

areas and farmers issues”, formed a situation of multilateral

cooperation and full participation by all members, thus expanded and

deepened the functions of Chinese futures market to serve the real

economy. ZCE successfully organized the first “Zhengzhou

Agricultural Futures (Cotton) Forum”, achieved positive results on

further cultivation of existing commodities and promotion of market

functions. It also intensified front-line market surveillance, guaranteed

the smooth running of the futures market.

Wheat Futures

2010 年小麦期货市场运行平稳，市场功能发挥良好。优质强

筋小麦期货共成交合约 580.46 万手，成交金额 1497.67 亿元；硬

现货企业利用小麦期货价格指导经营，进行套期保值，实现了稳

健经营。小麦期货在服务“三农”，促进农业产业化方面发挥了积极

作用。

In 2010, the wheat futures market developed smoothly，market

functions were well exercised. The annual total trading volume andturnover of Strong Gluten Wheat futures reached 5.8046m contracts

and 149.767bn Yuan. The annual total trading volume and turnover of

Hard White Winter Wheat futures reached 35,100 contracts and

0.762bn Yuan. Throughout the whole year, ZCE wheat futures price

increased steadily, which reflected the market conditions and the

purpose of macroeconomic adjustment by the state government.

Numerous enterprises within spot market referred to wheat futures

prices as a reference and hedged against the spot market, which helped

them to achieve stable operations. Wheat futures also brought positive

effects on serving the “Agriculture, Rural areas and Farmers Issues” as

well as helping with the industrialization of agricultural industry.
