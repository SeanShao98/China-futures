改革创新 建立现代化的农产品期货市场

神猴欢跃辞旧岁，金鸡高歌报新年。 在此， 我们代表郑州商品交

易所（以下简称郑商所）向广大会员单位和一直关心、支持郑州期货

市场发展的各界人士表示衷心感谢。

2004 年，郑商所坚持依法监管、规范运作,小麦和棉花期货交易

平稳运行，新品种开发、制度和技术创新工作不断取得新成效。

小麦、棉花期货市场在促进我国农业种植结构调整、增加农民收

入等方面的积极作用开始发挥。

2005 年，郑商所将按照《国务院关于推进资本市场改革开放和

稳定发展的若干意见》的精神，规范运作、严控风险，开拓创新，实

现稳步发展。要加大市场开发力度， 进一步活跃小麦和棉花期货交易；

要围绕服务国民经济特别是中西部经济发展的方向，建设粮、棉、油、

糖等重要农产品的现代化期货市场；要做好白砂糖、小麦期权、油菜

籽等品种的上市准备工作，推动新品种早日上市交易；要加快创新步

伐，通过制度、技术创新打造先进的技术平台，提高市场效率；要提

高服务水平， 降低会员和投资者交易成本；要做好新品种研究开发工

作，为建设现代化期货市场奠定基础。

我们坚信，在中国证监会领导下，通过与广大会员共同努力，郑

商所以粮、棉、油、糖为主要品种的农产品期货市场，将逐步成为全

国小麦、棉花、白糖、油料等农产品的交易中心、价格中心和信息中心。

总经理 王献立 理事长 张学仁

**THE LETTER FROM CHAIRMAN AND PRESIDENT**

In the coming new year, on behalf of the Zhengzhou Commodity Exchange

(hereinafter referred to ZCE), we would like to express our sincere thanks to the

member firms, investors and people from all circles who have been consistently

supporting Zhengzhou futures market.

In 2004, ZCE persisted on supervising and administrating businesses according

to relevant regulations. Wheat and cotton futures trades were smoothly operated. We

constantly won the success in new products development and innovations of system

and technology. Wheat and cotton futures markets started to play their role in

promoting agricultural farming structure and increasing farmers’ income.

This year, ZCE will adhere to the spirit, set by the State Council, of promoting

the reformation and opening to the outside world and the stable development of

Chinese capital markets, regulating operations, controlling risks and trying to pioneer

so as to achieve a stable development. We will enhance marketing and further enliven

wheat and cotton futures trading. We will provide good service to the development of

the national economy and the middle-west economy, and construct a modernized

futures market with grain, cotton, edible oil, sugar and other agricultural products. In

the meantime, we will make everything ready for listing contracts of fine sugar,

options on wheat futures and rapeseed. And at last, we will put up an advanced

technological platform, increase market efficiency and service level, and decrease

trading costs of the member firms and investors.

We firmly believe that, under the leadership of China Securities Regulatory

Commission and with great efforts of ZCE and its members and investors, ZCE willbecome trading center, price center and information center for the wheat, cotton, fine

sugar, edible oil and some agricultural products in China.

President Chairman
